    Summer Science Seminars

    Learn More
  • A lecture Splash-like marathon bringing to you knowledge about various branches of science!
  • Teachers: Sam Wang, Stephanie Wan, Owen Jiang, Nishtha Chhabra, Jerry Xu, Damon Gurvich, Nalini Datta, Ben Yin (Rising 11th), Caroline Ehmann, Jai Bhalla, Evin Liang, Eric Zhang, Bella Yu, Angie Zhang
  • General Topics: Various
  • Format: Synchronous lectures (online via Zoom)
  • Recommended for: Rising 5th-9th graders (exceptional 4th graders on a case-by-case basis)
  • Cost: $15/day attended, 20% discount if all days are attended (that's $60), office hours and the first and last session are free as long as you register for another class
  • Registration Deadline: Sunday, July 16th, 2023 at 11:59 PM EDT
  • Schedule: Monday, July 17th, 2023 to Friday, July 21st, 2023, 9:00 am - 5:00 pm and 7:00 pm - 8:00 pm, hour-long blocks
    • Register!

    Slice of Art

    Learn More
  • Covering the basics of art and their purposes, getting you introduced to multiple mediums!
  • Teachers: Nishtha Chhabra and Crystal Zheng
  • Mediums: Digital Art (Procreate), Sketching, Soft Pastels, Acrylic
  • Format: In-person
  • Recommended for: Rising 3rd-8th graders
  • Cost: Digital Art (Procreate) and Sketching: $15/hr, Soft Pastels and Acrylic: $20/hr
    A 15% off discount will be applied for purchase of 6+ classes (can mix between different mediums)
  • Registration Deadline: None
  • Schedule: Weekdays from Monday, July 17th, 2023 to Friday, August 18th, 2023
    Digital Art (Procreate) and Sketching: Mon, Tue, Wed, 4pm - 5pm
    Soft Pastels and Acrylic: Wed, Thu, Fri, 5:30pm - 6:30 pm
  • Location: Will be communicated to you upon registration
    • Register!
