Our Hopkinton branch is an extension of the Lexington program, which has proven to be highly successful since the Summer of 2020 with over 100 students in 2023. The Hopkinton branch provides access to the Science Outdoors program for students in Hopkinton and surrounding towns. The Science Outdoors program provides an opportunity for students to learn about different Science related topics outdoors and with other students.
