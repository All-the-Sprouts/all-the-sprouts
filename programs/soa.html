Slice of Art

Welcome to Slice of Art!

Humans need to express themselves and relax; art is an excellent mechanism to do so. Fostering ideas, processes, and thinking methods of art would help people discover the joy of embracing imagination.

We aim to cover the basics of art and their purpose. By taking the classes you can enhance your understanding of art while getting introduced to multiple mediums. We can also help you learn about different competitions, examples are Google Doodle and Scholastics awards.

Your Teachers

  • Nishtha Chhabra: Digital
  • Crystal Zheng: In-Person

Offered Mediums: Sketching, Pastels, Acrylic, Oils

Program Format: Classes are open for all of summer with each session lasting one hour. Please see the class schedule in the calendar below to decide on your personal class time. Digital Art will be held online, while other mediums will be held in-person.

Requirements: Prerequisites include

  • Being a rising 2nd-8th grader
  • Bringing a sketchbook (type of your choice), pencils (HB, 5B and 2HB), an eraser, and a blending stump if you are sketching
  • Bringing a sketchbook and pastels (soft or oil, depending on which you would like to learn) if you will be learning pastels
  • Bring a canvas (size of your choice) and brushes (size 2, 4, 8, and 10) if you will be learning acrylic
  • Being interested hoping to engage in art!

Costs: $10 for online, $15 for in-person
