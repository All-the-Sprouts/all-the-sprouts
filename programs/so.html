Science Outdoors

Welcome to Science Outdoors!

Science Outdoors is a free summer program to introduce incoming middle schoolers and freshmen to various STEM subjects thorough hands-on introductory classes.

Your Teachers:

  • Nishtha Chhabra
  • Owen Jiang
  • Erin Suh
  • Stephanie Wan

Offered Topics: TBD

Program Format: This program will be held on-location. Twelve students will be accepted. During lessons, teachers will give talks and students will occasionally have worksheets to help them follow along. If you have previously participated, we recommend attending Summer Science Seminars!

Requirements: Although we believe anyone can learn something at our programs, we are human and thus cannot give all who apply adequate attention. As such, we choose around 10 students each running. Prequisites include

  • Being a rising 5th-9th grader
  • Having enthusiasm for science and be able to show us why
  • Being excited to learn!

Costs: This program is FREE!
