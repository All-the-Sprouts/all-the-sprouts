Science Outdoors

Welcome to Science Outdoors!

Science Outdoors is a free summer program that focuses on learning in a natural environment. The program provides students with hands-on activities, exploration, and scientific inquiry in an outdoor setting. We aim to introduce incoming middle schoolers and freshmen to various STEM subjects thorough various introductory classes.

Your Teachers:

  • Nishtha Chhabra
  • Owen Jiang
  • Stephanie Wan

General Topics: Introductory Physics, Biology, Chemistry, and Earth Science

Format: This program will be held on-location. Ten students will be accepted. During lessons, teachers will give talks and students will be exposed to an interactive and fun experience. If you have previously participated, we recommend attending Summer Science Seminars!

Requirements: Although we believe anyone can learn something at our programs, we are human and thus cannot give all who apply adequate attention. As such, we choose around 10 students each running. Prequisites include

  • Being a rising 6th-9th grader
  • Having enthusiasm for science and be able to show us why
  • Being excited to learn!

Cost: This program is FREE!

Registration Deadline: Wednesday, July 5th, 2023 at 11:59 PM EDT

Schedule: Monday, August 21st, 2023 to Friday, August 25th, 2023, 9:00 am - 12:00 pm

Location: Lexington Community Center Tent at 39 Marrett Rd, Lexington, MA 02421
