SOA Art Competition 2023

We're glad you're here to give it your best!

The Slice of Art Art Competition (yes, we know it's tautological) is a chance for you to try your hand at an interpretation of an artistic theme. Your art will be judged on artistic skills, creativity, and originality.

This time, the theme is mental health. Your interpretation is up to you: the world is your oyster!

Winners will be announced on April 1st, on our website and through emails to the winners.

Rules

  • You must be in grades 3-8
  • Your work must be school-appropriate
  • Your work must be original (references are OK, but you must send a link to them)
  • Your work must include a short description and title
  • Do not include copyrighted material
  • Your form must be turned in by March 15, 2023 11:59 PM EST
  • DO NOT mail us your image - your work must be turned in as an image through the form below.
  • More rules here...

Prizes

  • 1st Place: Prize, Certificate, Display on this website
  • 2nd Place: Certificate, Display on this website
  • 3rd Place: Certificate, Display on this website
  • 1st Place, Elementary School Young Artists: Prize, Certificate, Display on this website
