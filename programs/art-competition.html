Lexington Sprouts Art Competition Fall 2023

The Lexington Sprouts Art Competition is a chance for you to try your hand at an interpretation of an artistic theme. Your art will be judged on artistic skills, creativity, and originality.

This time, the theme is personal connection to nature. Your interpretation is up to you: the world is your oyster!

Winners will be announced on November 10th, on our website and through emails to the winners.

Rules

  • You must be in grades 1-8
  • Your work must be school-appropriate
  • Your work must be original (references are OK, but you must send a link to them)
  • Your work must include a short description and title
  • Do not include copyrighted material
  • Your form must be turned in by October 30, 2023 11:59 PM EST
  • DO NOT mail us your image - your work must be turned in as an image through the form below.
  • More rules here...

Prizes

  • There will be five winners, all of whom will have their work displayed on this website

Judges

  • Stephanie Wan
  • Crystal Zheng
  • Nishtha Chhabra
