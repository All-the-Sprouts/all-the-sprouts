Welcome to Slice of Art!

It is important to express yourself and relax; art is an excellent mechanism to do so! Fostering ideas, processes, and thinking methods of art would help people discover the joy of embracing imagination.

We aim to cover the basics of art and their purpose for young children between the ages of 9 to 14 (rising 3rd grader to rising 8th grader)! Join us in this 5-week program to enhance your understanding of art while getting introduced to multiple mediums. We can also help you learn about different competitions, like Google Doodle and Scholastics awards!

Your Teachers:

  • Nishtha Chhabra
  • Crystal Zheng

Mediums: Digital Art (Procreate), Sketching, Soft Pastels, Acrylic

Format: Sessions are open on weekdays between July 17th and August 18th, each lasting one hour. Please have a look at the class schedule in the calendar below to decide on your class time. All classes will be hosted in person in Lexington, specific details will be available once you sign up.

Requirements: Prerequisites include

  • Being a rising 3rd-8th grader
  • Bringing your own materials (see the FAQ here for a list of what to bring)
  • Being interested hoping to engage in art!

Cost: FREE

Registration Deadline: None, rolling

Schedule: Weekdays from August 5th - 23rd, 2024, 4 pm to 6 pm

Location: Lexington Community Center Tent at 39 Marrett Rd, Lexington, MA 02421
