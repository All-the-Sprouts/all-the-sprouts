Welcome to Science Outdoors!

Science Outdoors is a free summer program that focuses on learning in a natural environment. The program provides students with hands-on activities, exploration, and scientific inquiry in an outdoor setting. We aim to introduce incoming middle schoolers and freshmen to various STEM subjects thorough various introductory classes.
