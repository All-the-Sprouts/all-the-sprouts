Welcome to Summer Science Seminars 3S!

WHO: We are a group of qualified and dedicated science enthusiasts who are eager to share our knowledge with others.

WHAT: We aim to cover a broad range of science topics and help you enjoy science, enhance your understanding, and prepare you for science-based competitions such as Science Bowl and Science Olympiad, and more.

WHERE: In-person demonstrations and team activities at the Lexington Community Center, all courses over Zoom

WHEN: Classes run from July 11 - August 6, specific times subject to change on a class-by-class basis, see Courses page

WHY: We believe the universe is to be enjoyed and understood, from the largest galactic superclusters to the smallest units of theoretical quantum foam. The ideas, processes, and thinking methods you learn will help you in life, no matter in science or not. We want to foster this in all students and help them discover the joys of learning and figuring stuff out. Every problem solved is a step and a challenge conquered.

We can't wait to show you everything!
